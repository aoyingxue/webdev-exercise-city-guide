Yuki's Favorite City: Amsterdam

Amsterdam is the capital and most popular city of Netherlands. Amsterdam's main attractions not only include historic canals, but also hundreds of different museums, coffee shops and the red-light district. It's well-known for its nightlife and festival activity. It is home for hundreds of world-famous DJs.
