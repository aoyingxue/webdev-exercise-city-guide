Yuki's Favorite City: Amsterdam

Amsterdam is the capital and most popular city of Netherlands. Amsterdam's main attractions not only include historic canals, but also hundreds of different museums, coffee shops and the red-light district. It's well-known for its nightlife and festival activity. It is home for hundreds of world-famous DJs.

Restaurants

Buffet van Odette

Description: This sweet soup-and-salad spot is set up right in the center of Amsterdam’s most notable sights and offers the kind of light-yet-nourishing breakfasts and lunches—including a robust salad bar where you can get heaps of fresh veggies for a good price—that fill you up without weighing you down. If staying late at the museums, the three-course dinner changes daily according to what’s in season. Weather-permitting, grab a seat at one of the outdoor tables.

Location Highlights:

  • Good for groups.
  • Good for kids.
  • Takes reservations.

Museums

Van Gogh Museum

Description: The Van Gogh Museum is a Dutch art museum dedicated to the works of Vincent van Gogh and his contemporaries. The museum contains the largest collection of Van Gogh's paintings and drawings in the world. In 2019, the Van Gogh Museum launched the Meet Vincent Van Gogh Experience, a technology-driven "immersive exhibition" on Van Gogh's life and works, which has toured globally.
